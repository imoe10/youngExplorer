Our story

At Young Explorer Nursery we will provide a high-end child care facility for children from 10 months to 4 years old. We offer a low teacher to student ratio, custom facilities, and innovative learning programs.

The children will be engaged throughout the day, learning new skills and reinforcing already acquired ones and we hope to create a nursery that has a reputation for providing an unparalleled level of care and is highly regarded in the community.

 Our values, namely Respect, Kindness, Fairness, Responsibility, and Unity, are reflected in our vision statement and expressed in a child’s own words, everyone “shares and cares”. We will work together to make decisions and help each other. We will always be loving, proud and happy with what we can achieve. We are a school where everyone follows their dream and never gives up. Our vision is to be a place where we are all happy and safe and have the courage to learn something new while caring for each other and our world.

Galil​eo 
04 - 12 Months

Meet our youngest explorers. Their area is a self-contained bright, comfortable and friendly space, with a soft padded floor to cushion those early walking bumps. Your children will be able to grow and develop in a safe and loving environment.

Marco 
12 - 18 Months

Marco is the next step on your child’s learning journey. As children become more physically active and in need of more challenging activities they will discover a whole new world of possibilities.

Polo 
18 - 24 Months

Working closely with Marco, the children will have plenty of opportunity to continue to explore their surroundings, try new things and grow and mature through play, working especially on their social and emotional development. Sharing is caring!

Livingston 
24 - 30 Months

At this age the children will be moving confidently, developing language and negotiation skills and flexing their independence in the choices they make. Building on the earlier support of Marco and Polo the children will continue their learning journey through play, positive reinforcement and the opportunity to explore many sensory experiences.

Cousteau 
30 - 36 Months

Our children will learn through play, songs and rhymes to recognise basic colour, shape, letters and numbers which will be incorporated into everyday activities and language. It is an exciting age where anything is possible and with our encouragement the children will make it happen!

Kingsley & Mackenzie 
30 - 36 Months

Our Kingsley and Mackenzie children will share a large, bright and challenging space, set out to cover the 7 areas of learning. Independence is a key word in this area and with a high staff to child ratio the teacher will have the opportunity to work with small groups on a topic based theme and be able to identify and support the needs of each unique child.

Curri​cul​​​um

Welcome to Young Explorer Nursery, the value and desirability of Nursery Education is widely recognised, not only on an educational basis but also to help a child develop social skills and self-confidence which can be carried throughout their life.

We pride ourselves at Young Explorer on being able to provide a safe, happy and dynamic Nursery experience for both the children and the parents to enjoy, knowing that their child is being cared for and helped along their unique learning journey by motivated, professional and experienced teachers and assistants. Children from 10 months to 4 years are encouraged to explore their full potential, developing at a relaxed pace that is challenging and rewarding, often messy but above all fun. 

Our play-based learning is taken from the British EYFS Curriculum, for more information on what to expect you are welcome to visit the nursery.
